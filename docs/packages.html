Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What makes these rentals eco-friendly?

All our properties meet strict sustainability criteria including renewable energy, water conservation, waste reduction, and local community support.

How does carbon offsetting work?

We calculate the carbon footprint of your stay and invest in verified offset projects like reforestation and renewable energy initiatives.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No minimum commitment required. You can cancel your membership at any time with no penalties or fees.
